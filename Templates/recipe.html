Quiche lorraine

30 mins
4-6 people
500/800kcal

Cooking notes

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Smoked salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium celery stick
  • 400g Vegetarian mince
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp za’atar spice
  • 1 stick sweet cinnamon
  • Whole nutmeg to grate
  • 250g Puy green lentil
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 vegetable stock
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 medium aubergines (550g approx.)
  • 568ml skimmed milk
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 30g cornflour

Cooking method

1) Vegetarian Ragu Sauce

Large saucepan/frying pan

1 tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, finely diced
1 celery stick, finely diced
Sweat until soft

Add

400g Vegetarian mince
2 garlic cloves (finely diced)
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp smoked paprika
1 tbsp za’atar spice
1 stick sweet cinnamon
Grating of nutmeg
Smoked salt and black pepper
Fry until mince is firm

Stir in

250g Puy green lentil
2 tbsp tomato purée
1 vegetable stock
400g canned chopped tomatoes
400ml water
Simmer for 20 minutes

2) Roasted Vegetables

Prepare for 30 minutes

2 medium aubergines (550g approx.)
Cut into slices (2cm approx.)
Score the flesh
Cover generously with salt
Leave to ‘sweat out’ bitterness for 30 minutes and pat dry

Oven bake for 30 minutes

Oil the aubergines
Place on baking sheet
Gas mark 7
Bake for 30 minutes until coloured

3) Bechamel

Saucepan, low heat

568ml skimmed milk
2 bay leaves
Grating of nutmeg
Salt to taste
Simmer until warm but not boiling

once warm

In a separate bowl add some of the warm milk and whisk in two eggs.

Remove the bay leaves from saucepan, whisk 30g cornflour with a tbsp of water and add to the saucepan

Simmer until thick

Once thickened

Turn down the heat and stir in The egg mixture.

Whisk until thick and combined

4) Assemble and bake

Spoon the Ragu into a deep oven proof casserole dish
Lay the cooked aubergines on top of the Ragu
Spoon the fluffy bechamel on top
Sprinkle flaky salt over the top
Bake Gas Mark 6 30 minutes
